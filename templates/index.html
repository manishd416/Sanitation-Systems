Welcome to the Clean Community Initiative

Empowering citizens to take charge of their environment. This portal is a dedicated platform designed to help local residents actively participate in maintaining public hygiene and sanitation.

How It Works

  • 🔍 Observe: Notice a sanitation issue like overflowing garbage, blocked drains, or water stagnation in your area.
  • 📝 Report: Snap a photo and submit the details through our secure reporting tool.
  • ✅ Resolve: Your reports are documented and can be forwarded to the relevant municipal authorities for quick action.

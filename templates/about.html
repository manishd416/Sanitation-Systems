Our Mission

Sanitation is the cornerstone of public health and a thriving environment. This initiative was born out of a Community Service Project developed by engineering students. Our primary goal is to bridge the communication gap between citizens and local administrative bodies through technology.

Our Vision for Chittoor

We envision a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable Chittoor region. By providing an accessible digital platform, we hope to foster a culture of accountability and civic duty, where everyone plays a part in keeping our streets and neighborhoods pristine.

Key Objectives

  • Digital Empowerment: Provide a user-friendly platform for tracking and reporting civic issues.
  • Health & Hygiene: Reduce the spread of disease by addressing waste accumulation and stagnant water promptly.
  • Environmental Protection: Encourage proper waste segregation and disposal methods.
  • Community Engagement: Inspire locals to take ownership of their surroundings.
